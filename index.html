Parametric Line Envelopes

This applet allows you to draw envelopes of families of lines, where the lines are defined by their endpoints which each have a parametric formula for x and y.

It uses JavaScript's evaluations of functions, so type "sqrt(a)" to take a square root or "a**b" to take a power. The test function can be used to show that a guess for the envelope is correct, and the parameter L can be used in your equations/ranges.

Type the points or ranges exactly as shown (e.g. "0,t")
